Glendalough

We had an amazing time exploring the vibrant city of Glendalough! From its ancient monastic site to its picturesque lake, there was so much to see and do.

Wandering through the 'Monastic City', we were awestruck by the towering round tower and the charming medieval stone churches. One charming stoned building included 'The Priest's House', a small charming man-made 12th century building. As we strolled around, we also couldn't help but notice the multitude of tombstones spread throught the path, each with its own story to tell about Glendalough's history and people.

The lakes were wonderful too! The tranquil waters were the perfect backdrop for spotting ducks gracefully swimming by. It felt like we were in a magical wonderland!

For more information about Glendalough and its attractions, you can visit their website: Visit Glendalough.
