Belfast

I had an amazing time exploring the wonderful Northern Ireland and historic city of Belfast!

I started out the day by exploring the Titanic Museum, where I immersed myself in the fascinating history of the Titanic's construction, marveled at the intricacies of its design, and learned about the lives of the passengers and crew on board. From the meticulously recreated interiors to the touching stories of survival and loss, every exhibit provided a glimpse into the tragedy and legacy of the Titanic.

Afterward, I headed to Dunluce Castle, an ancient ruin located dramatically on the cliffs overlooking the Atlantic Ocean. As I wandered through the weathered stone walls and explored the hidden chambers, I felt transported back in time to an era of knights, kings, and medieval intrigue. The breathtaking views of the rugged coastline added to the mystique of this historic site, making it a highlight of my journey.

Lastly, I finished off the day in Giant's Causeway,
