Kilkenny

Swipe through these images to see my experience in Kilkenny!

During our journey, we immersed ourselves in the medieval marvel that is Kilkenny. From its iconic castle to its awe-inspiring cathedrals and labyrinthine streets, every corner of this historic city whispered tales of a bygone era. Kilkenny's rich history and charming atmosphere made it an unforgettable highlight of my Ireland trip!

For more information about Kilkenny and its attractions, you can visit their website: Visit Kilkenny.
